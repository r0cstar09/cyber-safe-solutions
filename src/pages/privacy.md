## Introduction

Cyber Safe Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website cybersafesolutions.ca or engage our services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you interact with our website or services, you may provide:
- **Contact Information**: Name, email address, phone number, company name
- **Communication Data**: Messages sent through contact forms, emails, or during consultations
- **Business Information**: Details about your business, operations, and automation needs
- **Account Credentials**: Access to platforms required for implementing automation services (handled securely)

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent, referring sites
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session data and preferences (see Cookies section below)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- **Provide Services**: Deliver automation consulting and implementation services
- **Communication**: Respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, provide support
- **Service Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve our website and services
- **Marketing**: Send relevant information about our services (with your consent)
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet legal obligations and protect our legal rights

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

### Service Providers
Third-party vendors who assist in operating our business:
- **Web3Forms**: Contact form submissions (see their privacy policy at web3forms.com)
- **Calendly**: Appointment scheduling (see their privacy policy at calendly.com)
- **Hosting Providers**: Website hosting and infrastructure
- **Email Services**: Business communication

These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and process data only as instructed.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by:
- Legal process or government request
- Enforcement of our Terms of Service
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Investigation of fraud or security issues

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:
- Secure HTTPS encryption for website communications
- Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security reviews and updates
- Secure handling of platform access credentials

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your information:
- **Client Data**: For the duration of our service relationship and as required for tax and legal purposes (typically 7 years)
- **Marketing Data**: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
- **Website Analytics**: Typically 12-24 months

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- **Opt-Out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
